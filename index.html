Banff National Park

Banff National Park: Banff National Park is a popular tourist destination in Alberta, renowned for its stunning natural beauty and outdoor recreation opportunities. The park features majestic mountains, crystal clear lakes, and diverse wildlife. Visitors can hike, camp, fish, ski or soak in the historic hot springs. Banff offers a unique chance to immerse in the heart of the Canadian Rockies. The park is easily accessible, close to the city of Banff and with good transportation options. Banff National Park attracts tourists from all over the world.

The Columbia Icefield

The Columbia Icefield is a massive icefield in the Canadian Rockies, with an area of over 325 square km. This accumulation of ice and snow is one of the largest south of the Arctic Circle and attracts visitors from all over the world. Visitors can take a tour on the Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure to experience the glacier up close, or engage in outdoor activities such as hiking and camping in the surrounding wilderness. The Columbia Icefield is located along the scenic Icefields Parkway and provides breathtaking views of the Canadian Rockies.

Jasper National Park

Jasper National Park is the largest national park in the Canadian Rockies and a popular tourist destination in Alberta. The park is famous for its pristine wilderness, diverse landscapes, and outdoor recreation opportunities. Visitors can hike, camp, fish, and view wildlife, or explore the historic sites such as Jasper Park Lodge and Athabasca Falls. The park is also accessible via the scenic Icefields Parkway, offering stunning views of the Canadian Rockies and access to other popular destinations within the park.and provides breathtaking views of the Canadian Rockies.
